How to use this page

This starter is built for QR-code traffic: quick to load, easy to scan, and simple to update. The layout mirrors the postcard look with a strong branded header, a rounded hero card, and a clear call to action near the top.

  • Lean into the more human, social feel of the printed card.
  • Use warmer colours, softer copy, and stronger community language.
  • This page can highlight stories, testimonials, or simple examples of what happens at meetups.
  • Keep the layout consistent with the other files even though the tone is more relaxed.

Why a separate page helps

The warmer palette and friendlier tone make this feel different from the conversation-hours series even before users start reading.

Possible content additions

Include a short note about who can join, expected atmosphere, and whether participants can drop in casually.
