Resources for improving your English pronunciation

Ship or Sheep

An excellent resource for anyone needing to focus on single sounds in English. Available in our learning centre.

English Pronunciation in Use

Includes an excellent section on English sounds that are commonly difficult for learners of particular mother tongue backgrounds. Available in our learning centre.

University of Iowa Phonetics Project

Google to find this one. Excellent for video clips and diagrams on how English pronunciation works in terms of physical movement in the face. Useful to all learners, and especially good for those who have developed an American accent in their previous learning.

Elements of Pronunciation

Old, but still one of the best resources if you are moving on to improving your rapid speech and need to understand weak forms, stress and intonation. Available in our learning centre.
