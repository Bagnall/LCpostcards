How to use this page

This starter is built for QR-code traffic: quick to load, easy to scan, and simple to update. The layout mirrors the postcard look with a strong branded header, a rounded hero card, and a clear call to action near the top.

  • Keep the same structure as the English page so editing stays simple.
  • Use the softer green palette to match the postcard family.
  • Let the hero answer the three main questions immediately: what, who, and how to join.
  • Reserve the main panel below for practical details and reassurance.

Tone of voice

Supportive, conversational, and a little less formal than a brochure page, but still clearly institutional.

Best use for QR traffic

This version suits a quick decision path: a short intro, one or two key details, and a CTA button above the fold.
